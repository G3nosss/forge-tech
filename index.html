Forge Protocols

1. Custom fit depends on the measurements you provide. Measure twice, print once.

2. The AI Architect provides technical estimates. Final fabrication time may vary by complexity.

Safety & Risks

Batteries: Lithium power is high-risk. Never charge props unattended or near flammable materials.

Optics: HUDs and LEDs look incredible but will reduce your visibility. Do not operate vehicles while wearing gear.

Hardware: Electronics generate heat. Do not modify the integrated cooling vents in the 3D structure.
